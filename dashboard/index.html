01 · Story

We ran 47 simulations before lunch. Only 3 broke even.

BizBotBash is a playground for people who want to actually understand how AI reasons about business decisions — inventory, pricing, supply chains, shelf space — instead of reading another thought-leadership essay about it.

Pick a dataset. Write a bot in Python. Watch it win, lose, or do something genuinely weird. Every decision has consequences. Every bug costs revenue.

  • Who — Builders at Project Lab, exploring AI.
  • Why — Theory is cheap. Simulation is cheaper.
  • For — Devs, analysts, students, curious humans.
  • Cost — Free. Open source. Bring your own bugs.
